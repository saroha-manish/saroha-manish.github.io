Customer Churn Prediction

Project Overview

At HSC, developed a predictive system to identify customers at high risk of churn, addressing a critical revenue-impacting issue. Leveraged machine learning models (Decision Trees, Random Forest, Logistic Regression) to detect churn with 88% accuracy. The solution enabled targeted retention strategies, leading to a 17% boost in customer retention and improved campaign effectiveness.

Key Features

  • Churn Risk Prediction: Achieved 88% accuracy using machine learning models like Random Forest and Logistic Regression.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Analyzed over 1M records and 200K complaints to identify key churn indicators.
  • Business Dashboard Integration: Delivered weekly insights to marketing and customer success teams.
  • Campaign Optimization: Enabled personalized retention strategies through predictive targeting.

Technologies and Tools

  • Programming Languages: Python
  • Machine Learning Libraries: Scikit-learn
  • Data Processing: Pandas, NumPy
  • Visualization & Dashboards: Power BI, Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Model Evaluation: Cross-validation, ROC-AUC, Precision/Recall

Problem Statement

Rising customer churn was directly impacting business revenue and long-term customer value. The organization lacked a data-driven system to identify at-risk customers early and implement timely, personalized retention strategies, resulting in missed opportunities to prevent attrition.

Solution Approach

Processed over 1 million customer records and 200,000 complaints to uncover top churn drivers. Built and optimized ML models (Decision Trees, Random Forest, Logistic Regression) with 88% prediction accuracy. Integrated churn predictions into dashboards, providing actionable insights to marketing and business teams. Enabled timely, targeted loyalty campaigns to retain high-risk customers.

Results

  • 26% Improvement in Churn Prediction PrecisionEnabled more accurate targeting of at-risk customers
  • 17% Increase in Customer Retention Resulted from data-driven loyalty campaign strategies.
  • Stronger Business Alignment: Weekly insights supported faster, informed decision-making by marketing and retention teams.
  • Scalable SolutionBuilt a model adaptable to future customer datasets and campaign iterations.

Challenges

Key challenges included dealing with imbalanced datasets where churned customers were a small fraction of the total, making accurate predictions difficult. Extracting meaningful insights from unstructured complaint data required intensive preprocessing. Ensuring business teams understood and trusted model outputs was also critical. These challenges were addressed through careful model tuning, explainable outputs, and stakeholder collaboration.
