Role: Data Scientist

  • Service quality was impacted due to poor visibility into user experience and delayed detection of network issues.
  • Developed a predictive system to monitor Quality of Experience (QoE) using MOS (Mean Opinion Score) estimation to enable proactive issue detection and network management.
  • Built and trained machine learning models—Random Forest, XGBoost, and LSTM—achieving 94% prediction accuracy for MOS, optimized for robustness across variable network conditions.
  • Integrated MOS predictions into live dashboards used by engineering teams for real-time tracking and early warning of network degradation.
  • Reduced customer complaints by 22% by enabling early alerts on service issues, and improved SLA adherence and user satisfaction through faster, data-driven resolution processes.
